Frequently Asked Questions

How can AI coworkers help me as a designer?

AI coworkers assist with repetitive and time-consuming tasks such as generating creative concepts, creating design assets, writing supporting copy, preparing presentations, and organizing project deliverables. This gives you more time to focus on creativity and producing high-quality designs.

Will AI replace designers?

No. AI coworkers are designed to support your creative process, not replace it. They handle repetitive tasks and provide creative assistance while you remain in control of the ideas, design decisions, and final output.

Can AI coworkers help with client revisions?

Yes. AI coworkers can quickly generate alternative layouts, update design assets, revise supporting content, and prepare multiple versions based on client feedback, making the revision process faster and more efficient.

What types of design projects can AI coworkers support?

AI coworkers can assist with branding, graphic design, website design, UI/UX, social media graphics, presentations, marketing materials, and many other creative projects across different industries.

What makes Ndovesha different from other AI design tools?

Ndovesha provides specialized AI coworkers that collaborate across different creative tasks instead of offering a single AI assistant. Whether you're designing a brand, building a website, or creating marketing assets, each coworker contributes its expertise to help you complete projects more efficiently.
