How AI Workers Use Credits

Credits are the operational cost of running your AI department. Each Worker consumes credits based on the capability deployed—light tasks cost less, full campaigns cost more.
💬 Text Chat 3
📝 Caption 3
🖼️ Image 40
📊 Presentation 100
🚀 Landing Page 100
🌐 Full Website 150
🎬 Video 600
💡 Tip: Clarifying questions from a Worker only use 3 text credits. Full asset credits are charged only when content is actually created.
