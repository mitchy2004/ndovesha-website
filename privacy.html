Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ndovesha collects, uses, stores, processes, protects, and shares information when users access or use Ndovesha.

Effective Date 22nd May 2026
Company NdovuCloud Technologies Limited
Platform Ndovesha
Regions Covered Kenya, EU, US & Global
On this page
    01

    Introduction

    Ndovesha is an AI-powered software platform operated by NdovuCloud Technologies Limited ("Ndovesha", "we", "our", or "us").

    This Privacy Policy explains how Ndovesha collects, uses, stores, processes, protects, and shares information when users access or use Ndovesha.

    By accessing or using Ndovesha, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

    This Privacy Policy is intended to align with applicable privacy laws including:

    • Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
    • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
    • Applicable United States privacy regulations
    • Other applicable international privacy laws
    02

    Information We Collect

    Ndovesha may collect the following categories of information.

    Account Information

    When creating an account:

    • Name
    • Email address
    • Organization name
    • Password credentials (encrypted where applicable)
    • Profile information
    • Billing information
    • Subscription information

    User Content

    Ndovesha processes information users upload, generate, connect, or submit including:

    • AI prompts
    • Generated content
    • Documents
    • Files
    • Images
    • Marketing materials
    • Website assets
    • Text submissions
    • Imported files
    • Workflow inputs
    • Configuration settings

    Integration Data

    When users connect third-party services, Ndovesha may access authorized information necessary to provide functionality. Examples include:

    • Google account integrations
    • Google Drive files and metadata
    • Social media platform information
    • CRM integrations
    • Analytics integrations
    • Automation platforms
    • External API connections

    Only information necessary to provide requested functionality is accessed.

    Usage Information

    Ndovesha automatically collects:

    • Device information
    • Browser information
    • Operating system information
    • IP address
    • Usage logs
    • Interaction data
    • Feature usage metrics
    • Performance analytics
    • Error reports
    • Session information

    Payment Information

    Payment transactions may be processed through authorized third-party payment providers. Ndovesha may receive:

    • Billing status
    • Transaction confirmations
    • Subscription information

    Ndovesha does not store complete payment card information unless explicitly stated.

    03

    Google Drive Integration & Google User Data

    Users may choose to connect Google Drive to Ndovesha.

    When users authorize Google Drive access, Ndovesha may access Google Drive files and related metadata solely to provide requested platform functionality.

    Google Drive data may be processed to:

    • Import files into Ndovesha
    • Enable AI-powered processing
    • Support workflows and automations
    • Generate AI outputs
    • Improve user-requested functionality
    • Process user-selected documents

    Users maintain control over imported files.

    Users may revoke Google Drive permissions at any time through Google account settings.

    Ndovesha does not:

    • Sell Google user data
    • Share Google user data for advertising purposes
    • Use Google Drive content for advertising
    • Transfer Google Drive data to unauthorized third parties

    Google user data is accessed only to provide or improve user-requested platform functionality.

    Google Workspace APIs are not used to develop, improve, or train generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models except where explicitly permitted by users and applicable policies.

    04

    How We Use Information

    Ndovesha uses information to:

    • Provide platform functionality
    • Generate AI-powered outputs
    • Process user requests
    • Operate integrations
    • Improve platform performance
    • Secure accounts and systems
    • Prevent fraud and abuse
    • Provide customer support
    • Deliver subscriptions
    • Maintain infrastructure
    • Improve AI systems where legally permitted
    • Monitor reliability and performance
    • Conduct analytics
    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Communicate service updates
    05

    AI Processing & Automated Systems

    Ndovesha is built on artificial intelligence technologies.

    User information and submitted content may be processed by:

    • Artificial intelligence systems
    • Machine learning systems
    • Workflow automation systems
    • External AI infrastructure providers
    • Content generation technologies

    AI-generated outputs may involve automated processing.

    Users remain responsible for reviewing AI-generated outputs before publication or reliance.

    Ndovesha implements safeguards intended to protect processed information.

    06

    Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

    Where GDPR applies, Ndovesha processes information under legal bases including:

    • User consent
    • Contractual necessity
    • Compliance with legal obligations
    • Legitimate business interests
    • Protection against fraud or abuse

    Users may withdraw consent where applicable.

    Withdrawal does not affect prior lawful processing.

    07

    Information Sharing & Disclosure

    Ndovesha does not sell personal information.

    Information may be shared only where necessary with:

    Service Providers

    Including:

    • Cloud infrastructure providers
    • Payment providers
    • Analytics providers
    • Security providers
    • Customer support systems
    • Authentication systems
    • Integration providers
    • AI infrastructure providers

    Legal Requirements

    Information may be disclosed when necessary to:

    • Comply with law
    • Respond to legal requests
    • Protect users
    • Protect Ndovesha rights
    • Investigate abuse
    • Prevent fraud

    Business Transfers

    Information may transfer during:

    • Corporate restructuring
    • Acquisition
    • Merger
    • Asset transfer

    Applicable privacy protections remain in place.

    08

    International Data Transfers

    Ndovesha operates globally.

    Information may be processed or stored outside a user's country.

    Cross-border processing may occur where infrastructure providers, integrations, AI providers, or service systems operate internationally.

    Where required by law, Ndovesha implements safeguards intended to protect transferred information.

    09

    Data Retention

    Ndovesha retains information only as long as reasonably necessary for:

    • Platform operation
    • Security
    • Legal compliance
    • Dispute resolution
    • Service delivery

    Retention periods vary depending on:

    • User settings
    • Legal obligations
    • Platform requirements
    • Operational needs

    Users may request deletion where permitted by law.

    10

    Security Measures

    Ndovesha implements reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards including:

    • Access controls
    • Encryption measures
    • Authentication protections
    • Infrastructure monitoring
    • Security reviews
    • Abuse detection systems

    No technology platform can guarantee absolute security.

    Users remain responsible for protecting credentials and connected accounts.

    11

    Cookies & Tracking Technologies

    Ndovesha may use:

    • Cookies
    • Session technologies
    • Analytics tools
    • Performance monitoring technologies
    • Authentication technologies

    These technologies help:

    • Maintain platform functionality
    • Improve performance
    • Secure systems
    • Understand feature usage

    Users may control cookies through browser settings.

    Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

    12

    User Rights

    Depending on jurisdiction, users may have rights including:

    • Access — Request copies of information.
    • Correction — Request inaccurate information corrections.
    • Deletion — Request deletion where legally permitted.
    • Restriction — Request limits on processing.
    • Data Portability — Request information in transferable formats.
    • Objection — Object to certain processing activities.
    • Consent Withdrawal — Withdraw consent where applicable.

    Requests may be submitted to: help@ndovesha.com

    Identity verification may be required.

    13

    Children's Privacy

    Ndovesha is not intended for individuals under eighteen (18) years old.

    Ndovesha does not knowingly collect information from children.

    If information is identified as improperly collected, reasonable steps will be taken to remove it.

    14

    Third-Party Services

    Ndovesha integrates with third-party services.

    Third-party services maintain separate privacy policies.

    Users should review applicable third-party policies.

    Ndovesha is not responsible for external privacy practices.

    15

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    Ndovesha may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

    Updated versions become effective upon publication unless otherwise stated.

    Continued platform use following updates constitutes acknowledgment of revised terms.
