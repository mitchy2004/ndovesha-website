What you need to know about AI workers

Do I need to download an app to deploy AI Workers?

No. Ndovesha runs in your browser on desktop or mobile. Deploy your AI department from anywhere, with no app store required.

How do credits work across AI workers?

Each AI Worker consumes credits based on the capability you deploy. A Copy Worker executing a caption uses fewer credits than a Web App Worker deploying a full application. See our pricing page for operational costs across your department.

Do my department credits reset every month?

Yes. Credits included in your plan reset monthly, like operational budget for your AI department. Extra credits you purchase separately never expire, so you can scale capability anytime. On the Starter plan, 1,000 credits reset each month; any additional 500 you buy stay available until used.

Does my Website Worker's output stay live after my plan expires?

Yes. Sites deployed by your Website Worker remain live even after your plan expires.

If you connected a custom domain during your active subscription, the site switches back to a free Ndovesha subdomain once the plan expires.

Example:
While your plan is active: https://voltdriveke.co.ke/
After your plan expires: https://volt-drive.sites.ndovesha.ai/

Your capability stays online. Custom domain features require an active plan.
