Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI coworker?

An AI coworker is a specialized digital teammate that helps with specific business tasks, such as building websites, creating content, designing graphics, following up with leads, or answering customer questions. You assign the work, and your AI coworker helps you complete it faster.

Will AI replace me or my employees?

No. Ndovesha is designed to work alongside you and your team. AI coworkers handle repetitive and time-consuming tasks, allowing people to focus on strategy, creativity, and customer relationships.

How do I assign work to an AI coworker?

Simply describe what you need in plain language, upload any relevant files if needed, and your AI coworker gets to work. No technical skills or coding are required.

Can I start with just one AI coworker?

Absolutely. Start with the coworker that solves your biggest challenge today and add more as your business grows.

What types of businesses can use Ndovesha?

Ndovesha is built for business owners across many industries, including retail, e-commerce, consulting, agencies, real estate, professional services, and startups.
