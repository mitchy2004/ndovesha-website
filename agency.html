Frequently Asked Questions

How can AI coworkers help my agency?

AI coworkers support your agency by handling repetitive and time-consuming tasks such as content creation, website development, graphic design, marketing materials, research, and reporting. This allows your team to focus on strategy, creativity, and building stronger client relationships.

Will AI coworkers replace my team?

No. Ndovesha AI coworkers are designed to work alongside your team—not replace them. They take care of routine execution so your employees can spend more time on high-value work that requires human expertise, creativity, and decision-making.

Can AI coworkers handle multiple client projects at the same time?

Yes. AI coworkers can assist with multiple client projects simultaneously, helping your agency manage increasing workloads, meet deadlines, and maintain consistent quality as your client base grows.

Can AI coworkers help us scale without hiring more staff?

Yes. By taking on repetitive tasks and supporting project delivery, AI coworkers enable your agency to serve more clients and complete more work without immediately increasing your headcount or relying heavily on freelancers.

What makes Ndovesha different from other AI tools?

Unlike general-purpose AI assistants, Ndovesha provides specialized AI coworkers, each designed for a specific role such as content creation, website development, design, advertising, and sales materials. They work together like members of a real agency team, helping you streamline workflows and deliver projects more efficiently.
