Frequently Asked Questions

Will my website or landing page still be accessible after my plan expires?

Yes. Your website or landing page will remain live and accessible even after your subscription plan expires. If you connected a custom domain during your active subscription, your site will automatically switch back to a free Ndovesha subdomain once your plan ends.

While your plan is active: https://voltdriveke.co.ke/
After your plan expires: https://volt-drive.sites.ndovesha.ai/

Your website content stays online, but custom domain functionality requires an active subscription plan.

Do I need coding skills to generate a website?

No. Ndovesha is designed so non-technical users can build and manage websites without writing code.

Does Ndovesha support SEO?

Yes. Ndovesha includes built-in SEO support to help improve your website's visibility on search engines. Based on the features shown, Ndovesha supports:

  • SEO Settings — Configure SEO options for your website.
  • Export Sitemap — Generate and export your sitemap to help search engines discover and index your pages.

These features are available to help optimize your website for search performance.

Can AI coworkers create different types of websites?

Yes. Ndovesha's AI coworkers can help you build informational websites, landing pages, ecommerce stores, and web applications tailored to your business or client requirements.

What makes Ndovesha different from other AI website builders?

Ndovesha provides specialized AI coworkers that focus on different website types and tasks. Instead of relying on a single AI assistant, you have dedicated coworkers that collaborate to help you design, build, and launch professional websites more efficiently.
