AI Agents

Learn how Ndovesha's AI agents work together to create websites, content, ads, and more.

What are AI agents?

AI agents are specialized systems designed to handle specific tasks. Each agent focuses on one job — like creating social media posts, building websites, designing visuals, or generating ads. They are not general-purpose tools. Each one is built to do its task properly.

How many AI agents does Ndovesha have?

Ndovesha uses multiple specialized AI agents depending on what you want to create. Current agents include: Social Media Post Image, Social Media Carousels, Ad Image, Caption, Landing Page, Website, Presentations, Social Media Post Video, Logo, Blog Articles, Flyers. New agents and capabilities are continuously added as the platform evolves.

What does each AI agent do?

Each agent is designed for a specific type of output. The Social Media Post Image agent creates branded social visuals, the Social Media Carousels agent generates carousel-style content, the Ad Image agent creates marketing creatives, the Caption agent writes captions and copy, the Landing Page and Website agents build web experiences, the Presentations agent creates slides and decks, the Social Media Post Video agent generates video content, the Logo agent creates 3 logo concepts together with matching colour palettes, the Blog Articles agent writes long-form content, and the Flyers agent designs promotional materials. Each output is handled by an agent specifically built for that task.

Will my content still feel human?

Yes. Outputs are generated based on your input and can be edited at any time, allowing your final content to remain natural, authentic, and aligned with your brand voice.

Websites

Everything about building and managing websites using Ndovesha.

How does the website agent work?

You describe the type of website you want, and the agent generates layouts, sections, content structure, branding, and visuals based on your business needs. The website can then be edited, customized, and published directly inside Ndovesha.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most first website versions are generated in less than 5 minutes, then improved through edits and iterations.

Can I customize my website?

Yes. You can adjust layout, copy, branding, sections, media, SEO settings, and overall structure to match your exact business needs using either canvas editing or chat-based editing.

Will my website be unique?

Yes. Website outputs are based on your inputs, brand context, goals, and business information — not generic one-size-fits-all templates.

Do I need coding skills?

No. Ndovesha is designed so non-technical users can build and manage websites without writing code.

Can I edit my website after it's created?

Yes. You can edit with AI, edit directly on canvas, regenerate sections, update content, and continue optimizing your website over time.

What website features does Ndovesha support?

Ndovesha websites support features including brand settings, AI editing, canvas editing, hosting, custom domains, chat sharing, file uploads, traffic count, instant publish, SEO settings, sitemap export, logo saving, chat history, and chat history search.

Can I connect a custom domain?

Yes. Custom domains are supported, allowing you to publish your website using your own branded domain.

Will my website or landing page still be accessible after my plan expires?

Yes. Your website will remain live and accessible even after your plan expires.

If you connected a custom domain during your active subscription, the website will automatically switch back to a free Ndovesha subdomain once the plan expires.

While your plan is active: https://voltdriveke.co.ke/
After your plan expires: https://volt-drive.sites.ndovesha.ai/

This means your website content stays online, but custom domain features require an active plan.

Does Ndovesha provide hosting?

Yes. Hosting is included, allowing you to publish and manage your website directly through Ndovesha.

Does Ndovesha support SEO settings?

Yes. You can manage SEO settings and export your sitemap to help optimize your website for search engines.

Can Ndovesha create landing pages too?

Yes. You can generate focused landing pages for offers, campaigns, launches, lead generation, and marketing funnels.

Do I own my website?

Yes. You keep ownership of your website assets, branding, and content created for your business.

Credits & Pricing

Understand how Ndovesha's credit system works and how you pay for what you create.

How does the credit system work?

Ndovesha uses a credit-based system where each action uses credits based on what you create. Simple outputs like text use fewer credits, while advanced outputs like websites or videos use more credits.

What are credits?

Credits are the internal currency used to generate content on Ndovesha. Every time you create something — from a caption to a full website — credits are used based on output complexity.

How many credits does each task use?

Typical usage is:

  • Text / Chat 3 credits
  • Captions 3 credits
  • Images 40 credits
  • Presentations 100 credits
  • Landing Pages 100 credits
  • Full Website 150 credits
  • Videos 600 credits

This means you spend more only when creating more advanced assets.

Do credits get used for every message?

No. Credits are charged when actual content is generated. Clarifying system questions use minimal credits (3 credits), while full usage happens when a final asset is created.

Do credits expire?

Credits included in your subscription reset every month. Extra credits purchased separately do not expire and roll over for future use.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can top up credits anytime so you can continue creating without interruptions, especially during active campaigns or multi-asset production.

Can I buy more credits anytime?

Yes. Ndovesha allows you to purchase additional credits whenever needed, so your marketing workflow is never blocked.

Why is this better than paying per service?

Traditional services charge separately for websites, design, content, and ads. With Ndovesha, you use one system, pay based on what you create, and scale usage to your needs. You are paying for results, not time.

Do different agents use different credits?

Yes. Each agent uses credits based on its output type. A content agent (like captions) uses fewer credits, while website or video agents use more. This keeps pricing flexible and aligned with value.
